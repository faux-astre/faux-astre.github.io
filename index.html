About Me

I am a precision gem faceter specializing in custom cuts. I focus on maximizing light return and geometric accuracy in every stone.

My Process

Each rough stone is evaluated for optical properties, inclusions, and yield. I utilize modern faceting machines to execute specific index gears and angles tailored to the refractive index of the material.

Services

  • Custom faceting of rough material
  • Repair and recutting of damaged stones
  • Sourcing specific gem types for custom designs
